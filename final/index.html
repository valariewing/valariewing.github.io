Article Title

Aliens of all kinds are all over our galaxy. Some are not even from our own galaxy, but that's rather rare. Most aliens, however, are not friendly. Some are far more intelligent and seek only to use us or hunt us. We have encountered many on Earth. But in space, you are less protected. Here you can learn how to defend yourself, whether you are on homeworld or in the middle of space.
